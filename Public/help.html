Help & Guide

Main Menu

  • Start Race – Starts the race timer. Tap this when a new lap begins.
  • View Results – Opens a list of all recorded lap times. You can clear results or download them as CSV from here.
  • ⚙️ Settings – Opens the settings/more features page.

Race Timer Page

  • Tap to Record – Tap the screen each time a runner completes a lap to record their time.
  • Stop Timer – Stops the race timer. This must be pressed before results can be submitted.
  • Submit Results – Saves the race results after the timer is stopped.

Results Page

  • Clear Results – Erases all stored lap times.
  • Download CSV – Downloads the race results as a CSV file.

Settings Page

  • Scan your barcode – opens a barcode scanning feature.
  • Emergency help! – dials 999 in case of a health emergency.

Need more help?

  • Restart the app if something doesn't work.
  • All actions are designed to be tap-friendly and fast in offline conditions.
